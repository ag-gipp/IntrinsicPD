The program, which begins at Pattadakal and finishes in Aihole, is introduced by the Chief Minister of Karnataka. Artists, artists, artists and different craftsmen from everywhere throughout the nation partake in this occasion. In the 2006 festival, 400 craftsmanship troupes participated in the merriments.
