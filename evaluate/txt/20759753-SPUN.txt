Panchanagar 

Panchanagar is a town in Bardaghat Municipality in Nawalparasi District in the Lumbini Zone of southern Nepal. The district was built up on 18 May 2014 by combining existing Makar and Panchanagar VDCs. At the season of the 1991 Nepal evaluation it had a populace of 6238 individuals living in 1023 individual family units. This VDC lies close to the Churya Hilly Range.The occupants individuals who live here are generally Chhetri, Brahmin, Tharu, Magar, Newar, Dalits and Adhibasi. Most Youngest Nepali Football Team Star Bimal Magar was Born in Panchanagar VDC.
